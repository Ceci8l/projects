Wannawell - Concept Design Mobile App Project

Holistic Wellness Application
Wannawell is a digital wellness coach that guides body, mind and spirit to increase the level of life quality and achieve optimal health.

Responsibilities:
Built the brand identity (logo, typography, color system) aligned with the product’s purpose. Defined the value proposition and core messaging of the product.

Brand Value Proposition

01.Scope

This project focuses on designing a digital wellness experience that supports users in building sustainable and healthy habits. Wannawell is positioned as a holistic wellness coach, guiding users through personalized challenges, habit-building activities, and expert-driven content to improve overall quality of life. The goal is to create an engaging and supportive platform that nurtures body, mind, and spirit through intentional and science-based experiences.

02.Discovery Phase

Problem Statement
Consumers are in need of holistic solutions and sustainable wellness programs.

Solution Hypothesis
Wannawell's content for body, mind and soul provides users healthy habits for life.

Golden Circle

What
Wannawell is a digital wellness coach that guides body, mind and spirit to increase the level of life quality and achieve optimal health.

How
Wannawell accompanies users in their wellbeing journey via goal-oriented, personalised challenges, habit building activities, community support and scientifically-proven content prepared by experts.

Why
With 15 years of experience, Wannawell improves the quality of life through a holistic approach and creates healthy habits for life.
